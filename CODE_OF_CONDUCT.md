# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community. Be constructive, evidence-oriented, patient with different experience levels, and careful with security or private data. Harassment, threats, discrimination, doxxing, and deliberate privacy violations are unacceptable.

Maintainers may remove content or participation that violates these expectations. Reports should use the private channel described in `SECURITY.md` when they contain sensitive information.
